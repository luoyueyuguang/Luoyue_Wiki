- 离散卷积
$$
\vec{a} =
\begin{bmatrix}
a_{0} \\
a_{1} \\
\vdots\\
a_{m-1}
\end{bmatrix}
\quad
\vec{b} =
\begin{bmatrix}
b_{0} \\
b_{1} \\
\vdots\\
b_{m-1}
\end{bmatrix}
\quad \quad
\vec{a} \star \vec{b} = \vec{c} =
\begin{bmatrix}
c_{0} \\
c_{1} \\
\vdots\\
c_{m+n-2}
\end{bmatrix}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
c_{0} &= a_{0}b_{0} \\
c_{1} &= a_{1}b_{0} + a_{0}b_{1} \\
c_{2} &= a_{2}b_{0} + 
\cdots 
\end{aligned}
$$
